About Dropleaf Pro

Founded in 2008, Dropleaf Pro began as a small team of horticulturists and irrigation engineers passionate about solving the challenges of modern plant care.

Today, we've grown into a recognized leader in smart irrigation and foliage health, serving clients across residential, commercial, and municipal sectors. Our patented moisture sensing technology has revolutionized how professionals and homeowners approach plant care.

Our Mission

To develop sustainable watering solutions that promote plant health while conserving water resources, through innovation and scientific plant care approaches.

Our Vision

Gardening Tips & Tricks

Enhance your gardening skills with our expert tips and tricks for maintaining a healthy and vibrant garden.

Watering Wisely

Water your plants early in the morning or late in the evening to minimize evaporation.

Soil Health

Regularly test your soil to ensure it has the right pH and nutrient levels for your plants.

Mulching

Apply mulch around your plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
