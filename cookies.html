Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2023

At Dropleaf Pro, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to provide and improve our services. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to the website owners. Cookies are not harmful and cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your device. They simply serve as memory for the website, enabling it to recognize your device and store information about your preferences or past actions.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies for different purposes: strictly necessary cookies (essential for website functionality), performance cookies (collect anonymous data about website usage), functionality cookies (remember your preferences), and targeting cookies (used for advertising purposes). Each type serves a specific purpose in enhancing your experience on our site. We carefully review all cookies to ensure they are necessary and beneficial to our users.

3. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually set in response to actions made by you such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling in forms. You can set your browser to block these cookies, but some parts of our site will not work without them. Essential cookies help ensure the security and proper functioning of our website, including enabling page navigation and access to secure areas.

4. Performance Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us know which pages are most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and therefore anonymous. Performance cookies provide valuable insights that help us optimize our website structure, content, and performance to better serve our users.

5. Functionality Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages. If you do not allow these cookies, some or all of these services may not function properly. Functionality cookies remember choices you make (such as your preferred language or region) to provide a more personalized experience on subsequent visits.

6. Targeting Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other sites. They do not store personal information but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and internet device. If you do not allow these cookies, you will experience less targeted advertising. We use targeting cookies responsibly to ensure advertisements are relevant and useful.

7. First-Party Cookies

First-party cookies are set directly by our website domain and can only be read by our site. We use these cookies to remember your preferences, analyze site usage, and improve our services. These cookies are generally considered safer than third-party cookies as they are not shared across multiple websites. We maintain strict control over first-party cookies to ensure they respect user privacy and data protection standards.

8. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party cookies are set by domains other than our website, typically by our partners and service providers. These include cookies set by analytics services, advertising networks, and social media platforms. While we carefully select our third-party partners, we recommend reviewing their privacy policies as their cookie practices are governed by their own policies. We limit third-party cookies to those necessary for providing key website functionalities.

9. Session Cookies

Session cookies are temporary cookies that remain on your device until you leave our website. These are essential for proper website navigation and maintaining your session state (such as items in your shopping cart). Session cookies do not collect personal information from your computer and are automatically deleted when you close your browser. We use session cookies to provide seamless interaction with our website during your visit.

10. Persistent Cookies

Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period specified in the cookie (from minutes to years) or until you manually delete them. These cookies remember your preferences and actions across the site (or in some cases across different websites). We use persistent cookies to save you time by remembering your preferences and providing a more personalized experience during future visits.

11. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics cookies to understand how visitors engage with our website. These cookies collect information such as how often users visit the site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they visited prior to coming to our site. We use this information to improve our website and services. Google Analytics collects only the IP address assigned to you rather than your name or other identifying information.

12. Advertising Cookies

Advertising cookies are used to track visitors across websites and deliver ads more relevant to the user and their interests. These cookies are often linked to site functionality provided by third-party organizations like ad networks. While we don't show intrusive advertising, we may use these cookies for limited marketing purposes. Users can opt out of these cookies while still having access to our website content.

13. Social Media Cookies

These cookies are set by social media services we've added to our site to enable you to share our content with your networks. They may track your browser across other sites and build a profile of your interests. This may impact the content and messages you see on other websites you visit. If you do not allow these cookies, you may not be able to use the social sharing features on our site.

14. Cookie Lifespan

Different cookies remain active for different time periods. Session cookies expire when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain for longer (from hours to years), depending on their purpose. We carefully consider the appropriate lifespan for each cookie, balancing functionality needs with privacy considerations. You can see the duration of each cookie in your browser's cookie settings.

15. How to Control Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish using your browser settings. You can delete all cookies already on your computer and set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work. Most browsers provide clear instructions on managing cookie settings.

16. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can choose which types of cookies to accept. Your preferences will be remembered for future visits. You can change your cookie preferences at any time through our Cookie Settings page. We respect your choices and will only use cookies you have consented to, except for strictly necessary cookies required for site operation.

17. GDPR Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) regarding cookies and tracking technologies. For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we obtain explicit consent before setting non-essential cookies. We provide clear information about cookie purposes and give users control over their preferences. Our cookie practices are regularly reviewed to ensure ongoing compliance with privacy regulations.

18. CCPA Compliance

For California residents under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), we provide the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information, which includes certain types of cookies. We implement mechanisms to honor opt-out requests and provide clear information about data collection practices. California users can exercise their rights through our privacy center or by contacting us directly.

19. Cookie Settings Page

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through our dedicated Cookie Settings page. This allows you to enable or disable different categories of cookies according to your preferences. Your choices will be stored and respected on subsequent visits. We recommend reviewing these settings periodically as we may introduce new cookie types to improve our services.

20. Browser-Specific Instructions

Each web browser handles cookie settings differently. For Chrome, go to Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies. In Firefox, navigate to Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies. Safari users can find cookie settings under Preferences > Privacy. Edge users should look in Settings > Cookies and site permissions. We recommend consulting your browser's help resources for the most up-to-date instructions on managing cookies.

21. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies completely, some website features may not function properly. You may need to repeatedly enter information that would normally be remembered, and certain services may become unavailable. The website may also appear less personalized. We recommend allowing at least essential cookies for basic functionality. Our system will still work without non-essential cookies, though some features may be limited.

22. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature that lets you tell websites you don't want your online activities tracked. Currently, we don't respond to "Do Not Track" signals because no uniform standard for interpreting them has been adopted. However, we respect your cookie preferences as set through our consent manager and browser settings, which provide similar privacy controls.

Our audit process includes reviewing cookie purposes, lifespans, data collection practices, and third-party access. We update our cookie policy based on audit findings to keep users informed.

24. Cookie Security

We implement appropriate security measures for cookies that handle sensitive information. Secure cookies are transmitted only over encrypted HTTPS connections and often have additional security flags set (HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite). We carefully evaluate each cookie's security requirements based on its purpose and the sensitivity of data it handles. Regular security reviews help maintain cookie safety.
